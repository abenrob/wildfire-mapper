US Wildfires

2013-27-08 Source: Active Fire Mapping Program.

These data points represent current large fire incidents in the United States. Data are from the Active Fire Mapping Program at the US Forest Service. Individual points are shown while clusters of points are represented with the number of points close to each other. Hovering the clustered points show the bounds of the points while clicking them will zoom you in to the extent.

The hope is to keep this up to date with new publishings from the Forest Service. The maps being used are from Mapbox and the mapping javascript library is Leaflet.
